Biography

Phil Harris was born in Linton, IN on June 24, 1904. Voice actor who went from being on The Jack Benny Program on the radio to portraying Baloo in Disney’s The Jungle Book and Thomas O’Malley in The Aristocats. In 1933, he made a short film for RKO called So This Is Harris!, which won an Academy Award for best live action short subject. In his semi-retirement he made numerous guest appearances on Dean Martin and Bing Crosby’s talk shows. He worked as a bandleader for his father’s circus band. He later played drums in the Henry Halstead Big Band Orchestra during the mid-1920s.
