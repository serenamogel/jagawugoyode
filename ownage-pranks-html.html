Ownage Pranks - Age, Family, Bio

Voice actor and prank call master named Russell with an enormously popular YouTube channel where he performs prank calls as a number of different character personalities in which he created. His videos on YouTube would be watched more than 1.2 billion times. 

Before Fame

Prank calls started out as a hobby for him in 2004.  

Trivia

He created an app so people can download automated prank calls featuring characters in which he created. He briefly vlogged on a side channel called OwnageHimself. 

Family Life

He would choose to remain very private about his family life. His real name is Nabil Mady.

Associated With

One of the characters used in his prank calls was based on a character created by Dave Chappelle for Chappelle's Show named Tyrone Biggums.
