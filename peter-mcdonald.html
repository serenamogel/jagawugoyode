Biography

Peter McDonald was born in Ireland on January 28, 1972. Notable for his Irish Film & Television Award-winning performance in the 2000 movie Saltwater, he is also well known for his writing and directing work on the acclaimed films Pentecost (2011) and The Stag (2013). His television credits include roles in Moone Boy, Your Bad Self, and Sea of Souls. He co-starred with Brendan Gleeson in the 1997 feature film I Went Down. While studying English at University College Dublin, he was active in Dublin’s Fly by Night Theatre Company.
