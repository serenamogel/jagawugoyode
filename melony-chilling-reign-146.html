From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Melony (Japanese: メロン Melon) is a Supporter card. It was first released as part of the Chilling Reign expansion.

Card text

Attach a Energy card from your discard pile to 1 of your Pokémon V. If you do, draw 3 cards.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art ultra rare card, and as a Rainbow Rare in the Chilling Reign expansion, first released in the Japanese Silver Lance expansion. The Regular print features artwork by take, while the ultra rare and rainbow rare prints feature artwork by Taira Akitsu. The Chilling Reign Regular print was reprinted, alongside a Trainer Gallery ultra rare card with artwork by nagimiso, in the Japanese VMAX Climax subset. The Chilling Reign Regular print was reprinted again in the Japanese Start Deck 100. The VMAX Climax Trainer Gallery ultra rare print was later included in the Astral Radiance expansion.

The Chilling Reign Regular print was reprinted, alongside a Galarian Gallery ultra rare card with artwork by saino misaki, in the Japanese VSTAR Universe subset. This Galarian Gallery ultra rare print was later released outside Asia as part of the Crown Zenith expansion's Galarian Gallery.

Trivia

Origin

Melony is a Gym Leader in Galar. The interior of Circhester Stadium as it appears in Pokémon Shield is featured in the Chilling Reign Regular print's illustration.
