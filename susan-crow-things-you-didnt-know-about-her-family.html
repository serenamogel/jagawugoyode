Did You Know These Facts About Her Family &amp; Relationship

Susan Crow is an American woman who was a New York City schoolteacher in the past. She is also active in volunteering at several charitable events. Apart from that, she is widely recognized as the wife of Tony Bennett.

Tony is a legendary American singer who has a fan following all over the world. Till now, he has released over 70 albums which are all for Columbia Records. 

He is also an author by profession who has written five books in total and has great feedback from the readers. Now, Let's know about her life and family.

Early Life and Career

The beautiful woman, Susan was born on 9th September 1966 in California, United States. Her father is Dayl Crow and her mother is Marion Crow. From Fordham University she graduated and later joined Columbia University's Teacher College. 

Being a bright student she interned for President's Advance Office. Later she enrolled in her career as a teacher. Later she founded Creative Artists Management and served as an administrative assistant. 

Husband, Tony Bennett is Forty Years Older Than Her

The current Mr and Mrs Bennett are living a happy and wonderful life together. Despite having a forty years of age gap they still are very much deep in love. Interestingly, Crow's parents were fans of Tony where she grew up. 

Her mother even clicked a picture with Bennett while she was pregnant with Susan. In her childhood days, she even served as head of the Bay Area fan club for Bennett.

How Is Married Life Going On With Husband, Tony Bennett?

Susan and Tony encounter one another back in the late 1980s. They entered into a long-term relationship and enjoyed one another companionship to the fullest. On 21st June 2007 after being in a relationship for more than two decades they exchanged their wedding vows. It was a private civil ceremony that was held in New York. 

The ceremony was also witnessed by former Governor Mario Cuomo and many other individuals. As of now, the pair are in a wonderful relationship for more than three decades. They haven't any children from their relationship. However, Tony has four children from his past relationships.
