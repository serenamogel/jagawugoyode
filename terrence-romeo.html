Biography

Terrence Romeo was born in the Philippines on March 16, 1992. Filipino basketball player is known for his time with GlobalPort Batang Pier. He was named 2015’s Most Improved Player, winning the All-Star Game MVP and Three-Point Shootout along the way. He and his teammate Stanley Pringle became known as the Slash Brothers in the Philippines Basketball Association, a take on Stephen Curry and Klay Thompson‘s moniker of the Splash Brothers. He played at Far East University, winning UAAP Most Valuable Player in his last season with the school. He was drafted number five overall.
