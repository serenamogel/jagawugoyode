Schnacky Fam's Allison &quot;Allie&quot; Schnacky Is an Actress Turned-Influencer

Allison Schnacky (b. February 22, 2000), or Allie Schnacky, is an aspiring American YouTuber, actress, TikToker, social media influencer, voice actor, and dancer best known for her comedy skits, pranks, challenges, and lifestyles video on her YouTube channel with 546K subscribers.

Born in Orlando, Florida, to Lance Schnacky and Kim Schnacky, she has two younger sisters, Noelle Schnacky and Ella Schnacky, and an elder brother named Noah Schnacky. Besides, she also has an elder brother named Colby Schnacky who is possibly adopted as per the various news outlets.

Talking about her religious belief, Allie is also a Christian just like the other members of her family and is committed to active community involvement, and is heavily involved in her church as a junior worship leader. She studied marketing and communication at Valencia College.

As per estimating sources, Allie Schnacky holds a net worth of $1 million. She made her professional debut in the acting career starring as Chelsea in Gooney Bird Greene at just the age of six.
