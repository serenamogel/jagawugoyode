Jason King Derulo: Everything You Need to Know

Jason Derulo is a pop and R&B singer and songwriter who rose to fame with his hit singles such as “Whatcha Say”, “Talk Dirty”, “Want to Want Me”, and “Savage Love”.

He is also a proud father of a baby boy named Jason King Derulo, who was born on May 8, 2021.

In this article, we will tell you everything you need to know about Jason Derulo’s son and his relationship with his girlfriend Jena Frumes.

Who has Jason Derulo’s baby?

Jason Derulo’s baby is his first child with his girlfriend Jena Frumes, a model and social media star.

The couple met at the gym before the COVID-19 pandemic and started dating in March 2020.

They announced their pregnancy in March 2021 with a romantic photoshoot on the beach.

They also revealed the sex of their baby with a fireworks display in the Bahamas

What is Jason Derulo’s age?

Jason Derulo was born on September 21, 1989, in Miami, Florida, to Haitian parents.

He is currently 33 years old.

What is Jason Derulo’s baby called?

Jason Derulo’s baby is called Jason King Derulo, after his father.

The singer explained that he named his son after himself because he wanted his name to continue to thrive.

He also said that he considers his son his mini-me in every way and that he enjoys dressing him up like him and listening to music with him.

He also calls his son by various nicknames such as “little king”, “muffin”, and “cuddle bunny”.

How did Jason Derulo get famous?

Jason Derulo started his musical career as a songwriter for artists such as Lil Wayne, Pitbull, and Diddy.

He signed to Beluga Heights, a record label owned by producer J.R. Rotem, and released his debut single “Whatcha Say” in May 2009.

The song became a huge hit, selling over five million digital downloads and reaching number one in the U.S. and New Zealand.

He followed up with his second single “In My Head” in December 2009 and his self-titled debut album in March 2010.

Since then, he has released six more albums and numerous singles that have charted worldwide.

Jason has also collaborated with artists such as Snoop Dogg, Nicki Minaj, Jennifer Lopez, BTS, and Jawsh 685.

He has won several awards and nominations for his music, including five Teen Choice Awards, two BMI Pop Awards, and one MTV Video Music Award.

Jason has also ventured into acting, appearing in films such as Cats (2019), The Town (2010), and Night School (2018).

He is also known for his viral videos on TikTok, where he has over 50 million followers.
