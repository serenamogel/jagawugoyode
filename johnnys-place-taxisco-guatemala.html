Johnnys Place, Taxisco, Guatemala

GOJohnny’s WorldHeritageJohnny’s place story goes back to 1988 when a German pioneer named John Luttman decided to build a few guest houses on the Monterrico beach.
Originally built for friends and family, it quickly became Johnny’s Place Hotel due to the numerous demands.
SiteOn one of the nicest beach in Guatemala, between the Chiquimulila lagoon and the Pacific ocean you find Johnny’s Place.
The charm and laid back atmosphere is what made Johnny’s Place the most popular spot on the beach.
It is with great pleasure that we will guide you for your holidays in Monterrico and Guatemala.
