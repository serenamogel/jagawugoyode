Who is Alex Morgan?

Alexandra Morgan Carrasco, born as Alexandra Patricia Morgan on July 2, 1989, is an accomplished American professional soccer player renowned for her role as a striker. She holds the captaincy for San Diego Wave FC, a prominent team in the National Women's Soccer League (NWSL), the highest level of women's professional soccer in the United States. Additionally, she serves as a key player for the United States women's national soccer team.

After graduating early from the University of California, Berkeley, where she excelled as a player for the California Golden Bears, Morgan's talent was recognized, leading her to be selected as the number one overall pick in the 2011 WPS Draft by the Western New York Flash. She made her professional debut with the team and significantly contributed to their league championship victory.

At the young age of 22, Morgan displayed her prowess on the international stage, representing the United States at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup, where the team finished as runners-up. She continued to showcase her exceptional skills and abilities, particularly at the 2012 London Olympics, where her match-winning goal in the semi-final against Canada proved instrumental in securing victory.

In 2012, Morgan's exceptional performance earned her accolades, achieving an impressive record of 28 goals and 21 assists. This remarkable feat placed her alongside Mia Hamm as the only American woman to achieve both 20 goals and 20 assists in the same calendar year, and she became the sixth and youngest U.S. player to score 20 goals in a single season. Her outstanding contributions earned her recognition as U.S. Soccer Female Athlete of the Year and a finalist for the FIFA World Player of the Year award.

Morgan's career continued to flourish as she played a pivotal role in the United States triumphant victories at the 2015 and 2019 FIFA Women's World Cups. Her exceptional performance led to her inclusion in the Dream Team for both tournaments and in 2019, she was honored with the Silver Boot award for her contributions to the team's success.

Alexandra Morgan Carrasco's achievements and impact on the sport of soccer have solidified her position as a prominent figure in women's soccer, inspiring fans and fellow athletes alike with her exceptional talent and dedication to the game.
