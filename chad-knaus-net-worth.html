**Chad Knaus Net Worth**

Chad Knaus, Career and Net Worth

Chad Knaus was born on the 5th of August, 1971 in Rockford, Illinois, USA. He gained recognition as a crew chief in NASCAR, specifically known for his role as Jimmie Johnson’s chief in Hendrick Motorsports, in the Sprint Cup Series. Notably, he is the sole crew chief with five consecutive championships in NASCAR. His career commenced in 1991 and he has been associated with four different teams, including Melling Racing, and Tyler Jet Motorsports.

Knaus’ net worth is estimated to be over $5 million, as of mid-2016, stemming primarily from his thriving career in the sports industry as a NASCAR crew chief along with his endeavors as a NASCAR commentator. Additionally, his net worth has been augmented through his contributions to various video games, lending his voice for the character of a crew chief.

Early Life and Career Progression

Knaus’ passion for cars developed at a young age as he assisted his father, John Knaus, who served as a crew chief. He partook in several races, including the Rockford Speedway championship season. To pursue a career in stock car racing, he relocated to North Carolina in 1991, initially engaging with Stanley Smith’s stock car team. Shortly after, he secured a position at Hendrick Motorsports. His professional journey has been on an upward trajectory since.

Over the years, Knaus has held various roles, from a general fabricator to managing body construction and the chassis program. He worked as the car chief for Dale Earnhardt, collaborated with the Tyler Jet Motorsports, and later joined Melling Racing as the chief of the Dodge development team, notably achieving remarkable results with Stacy Compton.

Personal Life and Additional Contributions

Aside from his role as a crew chief, Knaus has ventured into commentary for NASCAR Performance and lent his voice to several NASCAR video games, including NASCAR 08 and NASCAR 09. Notably, he has faced suspensions by NASCAR officials at various points in his career, resulting in financial penalties that impacted his net worth. In his personal life, Knaus has been married to Brooke Werner, a former Miss Sprint Cup.

Conclusion

Chad Knaus’ dedication and contributions to NASCAR have fostered a successful career resulting in a substantial net worth. His impact extends beyond the racetrack, influencing the industry through his voice and expertise.
