Adam Copeland Tells Christian Cage To Go F*ck Yourself On AEW Dynamite

A big title match is set for next week’s AEW Dynamite.

The November 29 edition of Dynamite saw TNT Champion Christian Cage and Adam Copeland meet face-to-face in the ring as Cage addressed Copeland’s challenge for a match in Montreal next week.

Initially, Cage hid behind security guards and attempted a fake apology after claiming he did some soul searching. He then reflected on their memories together and brought up Copeland’s late mother wanting them to team up one more time.

When Cage went for a cheap shot, Copeland countered by hitting him below the belt. Clearly able to see through Cage’s bluff, “The Rated-R Superstar” finished the segment by telling Cage to “go f*** yourself,” which went uncensored on TBS.

Below is the updated lineup for the December 6 Dynamite:

  • : Christian Cage (c) vs. Adam Copeland
  • : Toni Storm (c) vs. TBD
  • MJF & Samoa Joe vs. TBD

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of next week’s Dynamite as it airs.
