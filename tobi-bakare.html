Who is Tobi Bakare? 

Tobi, from what little is known about him, was born in 1989, making him thirty years old in 2019. Tobi was born to Nigerian immigrant parents in London, England. Lizy Anjorin, a Nigerian actress and businesswoman, used to be his mother’s boss.

Tobi earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Hertfordshire University. Also, he received his acting training at Identity Drama School.

Tobi Bakare: Professional Life, Career 

Tobi originally aspired to be a professional football player, but he eventually decided to pursue a career in acting. His first appearance was in a single episode of BBC’s Holby City.

As a recurring character in another of BBC’s TV shows, Silent Witness, he achieved his first long-term goal. Following that, he appeared in several TV shows, including The Bill, Dani’s House, Casualty, The Shadow Line, and many others.

His breakthrough performance came in Matthew Vaughn’s spy comedy film Kingsman: The Secret Service.

Tobi’s most notable performance, however, was in the BBC’s crime drama series Death in Paradise, where he played the major role of officer JP Hooper beginning with the show’s fourth season in 2014.
