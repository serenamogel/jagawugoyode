Pidgeot (Japanese: ピジョットex Pigeot ex) is a Colorless-type Stage 2 Pokémon ex card. It was first released as part of the Obsidian Flames expansion.

Card text

Quick Search
マッハサーチ
 
Once during your turn, you may search your deck for a card and put it into your hand. Then, shuffle your deck. You can't use more than 1 Quick Search Ability each turn.
Blustery Wind
ふきすさぶ		120

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art ultra rare card, and a special illustration rare card in the Obsidian Flames expansion, first released in the Japanese Ruler of the Black Flame expansion. The Regular and ultra rare prints feature artwork by takuyoa, while the special illustration rare print features artwork by Jerky. The Regular print was reprinted, alongside a new Shiny ultra rare print with artwork by the same illustrator, in the Japanese Shiny Treasure ex subset. This Shiny ultra rare print was later released outside Asia in the special Paldean Fates expansion. In Japan, the Regular print was available as a Non Holofoil in both of the Starter Deck & Build Sets.
