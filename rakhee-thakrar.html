Why She Left Sex Education?

Rakhee Thakrar is an English actress who has appeared in Hugo Award-winning director Hettie Macdonald's movie Banglatown Banquet. She has also worked as the voice-over artist for the popular TV series Doctor Who: Time War. 

She rose to prominence after she portrayed the role of Shabnam Masood in EastEnders and Emily Sands in Sex Education. More importantly, she has also set a unique mark among the audiences as one of the most versatile British actresses. 

Thakrar Is of Indian Decedent 

Despite being born on February 29, 1984, in Leicestershire, England, UK, she follows Indian culture as her parents are initially from India. However, she has not revealed her parents' identities. 

Regarding her siblings, she was born the eldest child of her parents. She has two younger brothers named Shivam Thakrar (1991) and Anuj Tharkar (1997). Just like her, both of them were born and raised in England.

Thakrar Is Married To Vikash Bhai

Page Eight actress Rakhee Thakrar is a married woman who has exchanged her vows with Chlotrudis Award-Winning actor Vikash Bhai. Her beloved husband Bhai is a renowned British actor known for Limbo and The Stranger. 

The lovely couple tied their knot on September 2, 2017, with a grand celebration in the presence of their family members and close friends. Since then, they have been sharing a beautiful husband-wife bond and living together without any argument. 

Recently, Thakrar and Bhai celebrated their fifth wedding anniversary in a massive resort in England. Despite sharing the same roof for five years, they do not share any children. 

Did You Know? Although the stunning couple married in England, the marriage ceremony was held in a typical Indian culture. The ceremony was followed according to Hinduism.

Movies & TV Series 

Rakhee Thakrar, without a doubt, is one of the most successful Indian-Britain actresses who has significantly contributed to Britain's film industry. She made her first debut in 2006 by playing the role of Rozena in Banglatown Banquet.

Since then, she established herself as a talented actress and started appearing in many movies like Page Eight, Running Naked, 23 Walks, My Happy Ending, and Bollywood Carmen. 

Furthermore, Thakrar is more successful in TV series rather than in movies. Her notable TV series are Rules of the Game, Sex Education, Doctor Who: Time War, and Peep Show.

Due to her remarkable acting skills, she has been nominated for many prestigious awards like the "British Soap Award" and "National Television Award."

Thakrar Isn't Returning For Sex Education

If you are unaware of Sex Education, let us inform you that soon the fourth season will release. Unfortunately, we can not see Banglatown Banquet actress Thakrar portraying the role of Emily Sands in the fourth season of Sex Education as she left the show. 

She is the latest cast member to exit the show, along with Tanya Reynolds and Patricia Allison. Simon Ashley also left the show because of the agreements with Bridgerton, a romantic TV series.

When asked about this surprising decision, she smiled and replied:

I'm so proud of the show and grateful to have been part of something important. There is nothing terrible about Sex Education.

The reason behind Thakrar leaving Sex Education is still behind the curtains. Some stories believe that she denied playing because of financial issues between them.
