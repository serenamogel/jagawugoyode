WGTC Premiere: Tritonal I Feel The Love (ft. Ross Lynch)

Following up on their recent collaboration with Adam Lambert and Jenaux on “Broken,” Tritonal are back to share a new song titled “I Feel The Love” from their forthcoming album Painting With Dreams. The full-length was first teased earlier in the summer, and fans have waited impatiently for it ever since, with just a handful of teasers released so far from the upcoming record to satiate their appetites.

Recommended Videos

“I Feel The Love,” which we’re happy to be premiering today, features the kind of ear pleasing production that we’ve come to expect from Tritonal, opening with a serene chord progression and soothing vocals from Ross Lynch of R5. Light percussive touches soon join the mix alongside guitar strums, building up into an anthemic chorus that dives straight ahead into energetic drops with chopped up vocal melodies and driving four on the floor rhythms.

Tritonal have made a name for themselves as one of the most exciting acts to come out of dance music with their unique sound and gorgeous production values, and “I Feel The Love” is consistent with the qualities that have made them a success.

Painting With Dreams is set to be released this Friday and will be followed by a North American tour of the same name that will see the duo touching down in major cities like Los Angeles, New York, Toronto and more. We’ll have a full review for you on the duo’s latest effort in a couple of days, but until then, check out “I Feel The Love” above and let us know what you think of it by leaving a comment.
