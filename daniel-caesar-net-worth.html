Caesar’s debut EP “Praise Break” was listed No. 19 on Rolling Stone’s ’20 Best R&B Albums of 2014.’

Caesar released his first album, “Freudian,” on August 25, 2017, which includes the hits “Get You,” “We Find Love,” and “Blessed.”
