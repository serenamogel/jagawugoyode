How do you self clean a Kenmore gas oven?

Answer

How Do Self-Cleaning Ovens Function?

Before cleaning the oven, remove all of the pans and aluminium foil from the inside.

Remove as much baked-on food or oil from the surface as you are able to readily remove.

Close and lock the oven door.

Using the settings offered, set a time limit for the cleaning.

Allow the oven to cool when it has completed the cleaning cycle.

Using a wet towel, wipe away any ash residue.
