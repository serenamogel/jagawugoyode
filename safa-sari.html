Biography

Safa Sari was born in Istanbul, Turkey on June 22, 1992. Turkish actor best recognized for his recurring television roles. He is known for having starred in the sketch comedy competition series Cok Guzel Hareketler 2.Kusak as well as for having played Naim Kulunc in the 2019 feature film Money Trap. He starred alongside Olgun Toker and Demet Gul in the 2016 feature film Kucuk Esnaf. He started his acting career in 2015, making his television debut as Semih on the series Bana Baba Dedi. His feature film debut came in 2016 when he portrayed Dugundeki Muzisyen in the film Kucuk Esnaf.
